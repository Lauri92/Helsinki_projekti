Culture

Helsinki is a diverse seaside town with beautiful islands and green urban areas. It travels through its own, peculiar and unique rhythm through four different seasons. Here you will find both traditional Finnish culture and global city pulse.

Helsinki is the largest city in Finland, and the Metropolia area is populated by 1.4 million people. Despite its northern location, it's easy to grab a quick flight from Europe or arrive by water through St. Petersburg, Estonia, Sweden and the Baltic Sea. The lively water traffic and the sea waving in front of the city are definitely one of Helsinki's high-rise homes. Immediately out of town, the archipelago contains over 300 beautiful and a diverse island.

The rhythm of life in the Nordic countries is calm and approachable, literally. Public transport is one of the best in the world and with a little effort you walk or cycle around the center. An easy-to-use subway will also take you further away quickly.

Whether you want to adventure in the woods, enjoy music or enjoy a sauna -You've come to the right place.
