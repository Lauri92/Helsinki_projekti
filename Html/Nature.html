Nature

According to the National Land Survey of Finland, the area of Helsinki at the beginning of 2015 was 213.92 square kilometers or 21,392 hectares. According to a statistical publication, the city had a total of 1,898 hectares of parks and 4,734 hectares of forest in 2014, which means that about one third of Helsinki's area is different Green Zones. The city's oldest parks date back to the 19th century and new ones are being built all the time.
