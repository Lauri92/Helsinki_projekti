Uutela nature trail

"Uutela outdoor area is located in the southeastern part of Vuosaari. It was established between 1952 and 1953 and covers an area of ​​105 ha. The nature of the area is diverse and subtle. There is a variety of dry and fresh textile forests as well as bare cliffs."

Pornaistenniemi nature trail

"Pornaistenniemi's eight hectare tar alder groves are diverse in species. It was designated a nature reserve in 2016. 56 dwarfs and 99 orphan species have been found in the area, which is the highest studied in Finland."

Lammasaari Island

"Lammasaari Island is a protected island in the neighborhood of the Old Town, near Arabianranta. The island is a wonderful destination for outdoor activities and hiking. An unobstructed boardwalk from Pornaistenniemi takes you there, near the Old Town Walking Bridge."
